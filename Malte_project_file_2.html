Presentation and Information For Younger Students

This is designed to be a resource for younger classmen who are starting to consider and look into their futures. We have collected data from seniors who have recently undergone applying to college in order to help guide your own search and application process. Alongside data seniors have also provided fruitful tips, advice, and personal experience for you to reference as you look forward to the next phase of your educational career.
